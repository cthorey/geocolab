Welcome to geocolab

A attempt to simplify the AGU experience and networking in geoscience.

With nearly 24,000 attendees, AGU Fall Meeting is the largest Earth and space science meeting in the world.
Based on all AGU contributions and state-of-the-art natural language processing techniques, we design a recomendation engine able to extract relevant absracts throughout the 25000's submited to AGU.

Ever been lost in the scientific program ? Not anymore.

Based on our recommendation engine, we design an app that allows you too painlessly plan your AGU. Just give it a query or an abstract as a reference and it will propose you a set of interesting contributions to fill out your week with great science.

Find it hard to create your network ? Not anymore.

Based on our recomendation engine, we develop an app that makes networking in geoscience easy. Just give it a query or an abstract as a reference and it will propose you a set of people across the world that are working on similar topics.
